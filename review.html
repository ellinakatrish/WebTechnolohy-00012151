We all strive for height. We grow, we dream of a better life, we set goals for ourselves and make our dreams come true. The obstacle is only a test of our strength, whether we abandon our dream or fight for it.

The profession of a photographer is very similar to that of a hitman. The photographer is also looking for the right angle for a more accurate shot and is ready to go to great lengths for the sake of a quality result, but the photographer does not take life, but saves it, saves the moment on paper.

1 - Do not be afraid to take risks, take pictures of everything, take a thousand pictures and select two favorites, you will have experience, and with experience you will filter out the unnecessary and gain skills.

2 - Don't be afraid to work with light. The best shot is in the morning, it allows you not to use photomontage, makes your work easier and is more appreciated.

3 - Don't be afraid to take photos in dark light, learn to edit photos in apps and paint your own photos.

4 - Learn to plan a shoot, know where filming is not allowed, and quickly solve problems.

5 - Get ready to be a model. For people are afraid of attention but agree to be filmed. And then they require advice on posture, so it's important to be able to win people over to you.
